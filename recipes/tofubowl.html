Teriyaki Tofu Bowl

Description

This Teriyaki Tofu Bowl is a super-simple yet satisfying recipe. Crispy pan-fried tofu glazed in the beloved teriyaki sauce and served over fluffy rice with veggie. It has to be one of the dynamic one-bowl meals for vegetarians, vegans, and omnivores alike.

Ingredients

  • Medium-firm tofu
  • Potato starch
  • Homemade teriyaki sauce: sake, mirin, soy sauce, and sugar
  • Garnishes: chopped green onion and sesame seeds
  • Broccoli

Steps

  1. Press the tofu and make the homemade teriyaki sauce.
  2. Coat the tofu cubes with potato starch.
  3. Pan-fry the tofu and coat well with the teriyaki sauce.
  4. Serve the tofu with blanched brocolli over steamed rice.
  5. Garnish the tofu with the green onion and sesame seeds. Enjoy!
